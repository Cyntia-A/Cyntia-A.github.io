Unemployment & On-Demand Services

In Oakland, the unemployment rate is at 3.7% since of December 2017. Because of this some people often resort to on demand work services. What are on demand work services? On demand services are mobile apps that puts people's skill to use as a way for themselves to increase their income such as Uber, Lyft, Taskrabbit to name a few.

Underemployment is when people looking for work are not getting paid enough for their work or their job is not making full use of their skills and abilities. This could eventually leave the person living in poverty since they are not making enough money to support themselves. Therefore, people resort to working for on-demand workforces such as Uber or lyft, to fill in the needed income.

On-demand services can be a helpful tool in low income communities such as this one. Nothing in this world is exactly cheap-your rent is high, you have bills, insurance, and other necessities that you have to buy. Your job does not have a good pay either, so you're left with this financial situation.
